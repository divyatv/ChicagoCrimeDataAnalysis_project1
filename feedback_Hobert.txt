Project description
We will be analyzing crime data from City of Chicago from the years 2001 to present.This dataset reflects reported incidents of crime (with the exception of murders where data exists for each victim) that occurred in the City of Chicago from 2001 to present, minus the most recent seven days. Data is extracted from the Chicago Police Department's CLEAR (Citizen Law Enforcement Analysis and Reporting) system. 
Data Source: Data is extracted from the Chicago Police Department's CLEAR (Citizen Law Enforcement Analysis and Reporting) system. The dataset contains more than 65,000 records/rows of data and cannot be viewed in full in Microsoft Excel.
